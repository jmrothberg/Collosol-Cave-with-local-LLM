JMR's LLM Adventure

A fully LLM-driven text adventure running entirely in your browser. Gemma 4B generates the story; Stable Diffusion 1.5 paints the scenes.

Requires WebGPU (Chrome/Edge 113+). First run downloads ~4 GB of models (cached).
